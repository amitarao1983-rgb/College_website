MAYOR RADHAKRISHNAN COLLEGE OF PHARMACY

Delivering quality pharmacy education through academic excellence, practical training, ethical standards, and healthcare service orientation.

PCI Approved Institution University Affiliated Qualified Faculty Laboratory Facilities

Vision

To be a pioneer in pharmaceutical education, research, and healthcare services, fostering innovation and producing competent pharmacists to serve the healthcare needs of society.

Mission

  • To provide quality pharmacy education with strong theoretical knowledge and practical training in pharmaceutical sciences.
  • To promote professional, technical, and entrepreneurial skills required for the pharmaceutical industry and healthcare sector.
  • To develop responsible, ethical, and skilled healthcare professionals committed to patient care and community service.
  • To encourage research, innovation, and lifelong learning in pharmacy practice.
  • To collaborate with healthcare institutions and industries to enhance real-world exposure and employability.

About the Institution

Mayor Radhakrishnan College of Pharmacy is committed to excellence in pharmaceutical education, training, and research through a balanced curriculum integrating theory, laboratory practice, and healthcare exposure.

Principal's Message

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Mayor Radhakrishnan College of Pharmacy. The institution is dedicated to producing competent, ethical, and socially responsible pharmacy professionals through quality teaching-learning processes and modern laboratory infrastructure.

We promote innovation, critical thinking, discipline, and professional development while strengthening industry and hospital collaborations for practical exposure and employability.

Dr C.S. Saminathan

Academics

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm)

Duration: 4 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology / Mathematics

Intake: 60 Students

Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D)

Duration: 6 Years (5 Years study + 1 Year Internship)

Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology / Mathematics

Intake: As per PCI norms

Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm)

Duration: 2 Years

Eligibility: 10+2 with Science stream

Intake: As per PCI norms
